Kite Surfing Spain

Welcome to Kite Surfing Spain. We have established this page to share our passion for kiteboarding and other water sports all over the Spain. Spain has an amazing diversity of "spots" along it's coast lines to enjoy these sports. This site is mainly directed to kiteboarding and kitesurfing.
Our kite instructors are all IKO licensed and personally experienced with each spot. We are privileged to be able to kite 365 days a year in Spain. The further south we go the better weather in winter.

Marbella, Spain

KitesurfingSpain.com is based in Marbella the south of Spain right in between the Mediterranean sea and the Atlantic ocean, a short drive away from Tarifa "the mecca of Windsurfing" and now the fast growing sport of Kiteboarding. This site has been established to help all kiteboarders, kitesurfers, windsurfers, surfers and our future kids whether beginner or advanced, enjoy the beauty of water sports.
